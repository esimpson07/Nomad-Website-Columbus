About Nomad Arts

Our mission is to provide a platform for artists, musicians, and vendors to share their work with the community while fostering creativity and cultural exchange.

Our History

Nomad Arts began as a small gathering of creatives in Columbus, IN. Over the years, it has grown into a large event attracting hundreds of visitors, showcasing dozens of vendors, and hosting incredible live performances.
